About Us

Femprish is a non-profit organisation that is working towards getting rid of the negative connotations that accompany the word 'feminist'. We believe in equality for all, regardless of gender, race, sexuality, or any other factor. Our mission is to empower individuals and communities to fight for their rights and create a more just and equal society.

Our Programs

  • Leadership development workshops for girls
  • Career counseling and mentorship for women
  • Community outreach and education on gender equality
  • Advocacy and lobbying for policies that promote gender equality and women's rights
  • Financial assistance and scholarships for girls and women pursuing education or entrepreneurship opportunities
